About Collector's Edition

I’m Dylan Nguyen, a first-year student at Cal Poly and I’m from San Jose, California. Growing up, I became interested in the clothes I wore and to me everything in my closet is a representation of my identity as a whole. Fashion and streetwear has really influenced my life and has led me to find communities of people that share my interest as well. My wardrobe is constantly changing as new trends in the fashion world are always forming so my mission is to keep you up-to-date with what’s in and what’s out while also introducing you to some people I think embody what it means to stand above the crowd with your clothes.
